Introduction

Welcome to WatchWeeb. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS app and website.

Who We Are: WatchWeeb is an anime tracking application that helps you organize your watch list, track your progress, and discover new anime.

üìä Information We Collect

1. Account Information (Optional)

Sign in with Apple:

  • Apple User ID (encrypted, unique identifier)
  • Email address (if you choose to share it)
  • Name (if you choose to share it)

Guest Accounts:

  • Only a randomly generated local identifier stored on your device
  • No personal information collected
  • Data never leaves your device

2. Watch List Data

What we collect:

  • Anime titles you add to your watch list
  • Episode progress and completion status
  • Your ratings and notes (if you add them)
  • Timestamps of when you add/update entries

Storage:

  • Guest accounts: Stored locally on your device only
  • Signed-in accounts: Stored locally AND synced to your private iCloud account
  • Important: We do not have access to your iCloud data

3. Gamification Data

  • Your total XP (experience points)
  • Current level and progress
  • Achievement milestones

4. Technical Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and iOS version (for compatibility)
  • App version
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data (anonymized)
  • Image cache storage usage

Purpose: To fix bugs, improve performance, and ensure compatibility

üîí Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Lives

  • Guest accounts: All data stored locally on your device only
  • Signed-in accounts: Data stored on your device AND your private iCloud account
  • Our servers: We do not operate our own servers or databases

Security Measures

  • Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • iCloud data encrypted at rest
  • Sign in with Apple uses industry-standard security
  • Local device data protected by iOS sandbox security
  • No passwords stored (we use Apple's authentication)

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as you use the app
  • Deleted accounts: Data deleted immediately when you sign out and clear app data
  • Guest accounts: Data deleted when you uninstall the app or clear data
